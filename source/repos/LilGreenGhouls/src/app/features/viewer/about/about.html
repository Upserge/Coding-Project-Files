Our Mission 🎯

We're not trying to prove or disprove anything. We're just two friends who find the paranormal fascinating and want to share our experiences with the people we care about.

Every adventure is documented here — the creepy, the unexplainable, and sometimes the hilariously mundane. We bring cameras, audio recorders, and open minds to every investigation.
